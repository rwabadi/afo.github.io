FEBRUARY 21, 2024

Sojitz Corporation acquires Grupo Silaba

Sojitz Corporation, a Japanese general trading company, acquired full ownership of Silaba Motors, S.A. — founded in 1991 and Panama’s second-largest automotive group by annual sales, with a historical record of 170,000 units sold. The acquisition marks Sojitz’s entry into automotive distribution and retail in Panama, and closes the family’s 33-year chapter in the sector.
